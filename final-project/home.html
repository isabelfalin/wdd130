Snowboarding Throughout History

Snowboarding has evolved from a fringe sport to a mainstream winter activity, with deep cultural roots and a fascinating history. From its origins in ancient snow riding methods to its rise as a competitive sport in the Olympics, snowboarding has a story full of innovation, rebellion, and passion
